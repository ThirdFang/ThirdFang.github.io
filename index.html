A Little About Myself

Since graduating from Penn State Wilkes-Barre with my Bachelor of Science degree in IST, I've begun the process of gaining work experience. From my brief internship as an IT Representative at a restaurant, to working for the State of Texas as a Java prgrammer, I figure I've been doing decently well. As of writing this, I have intermediate knowledge of HTML and CSS, as well as a basic understanding of Java (primarily for android application development).

As I mentioned in the opening, I want to study and design video games, hopefully in the near future. This determination started back on my 9th birthday, when I received my first gaming console and video game (original Xbox and Start Wars: Knights of the Old Republic). I must have put a month's worth of time playing it, exploring multiple paths, story lines, moral choices, play styles, equipment combinations, team balance, etc. When I done all that I could do at the time, I knew what I wanted to do when I grew up: to design video games. It wasn't until high school where I realized what facet of game design I wanted to do, that being animation (I figured that If I studied animation and couldn't make it in the game industry, I could still use animation as another career path). My goal is to go back to school to study game design and animation (possibly art if that I need too), and eventually earn a degree in that field

When I'm not working, studying, dreaming, or scheming, I'm pretty much chilling and loafing about. Most of my free time is spent either playing on the PS4 (love it or hate it, I believe it has some of the best exclusive games), making and assembling handles for knives (will probably make stock removal knives in the future), creating car decals through media on the internet (specializing in video games, anime, and internet memes), and messing around with my cat CC (Crazy Cat/Cary's Cat, depending who you ask).
