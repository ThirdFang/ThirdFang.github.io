As I mentioned before, I want to eventually get into the game industry. That fascination was established way back when I was 9 years old, where I received my first gaming console and video game on my birthday. After devoting 70+ hours into it, exploring every possibility I could think of, I wanted to design games (I didn't know how to, but damn sure was gonna figure it out...right after I finish this level). My goal, when I feel I have grown competent in my current field, is to go back to school to study game design and animation, earning a degree in that field.

When I'm not working, studying, dreaming, or scheming, I'm pretty much chilling and loafing about. Most of my free time is spent either playing on the PS4 (love it or hate it, you have to admit it has some of the better exclusive games), making and assembling handles for knives, creating car decals through media on the internet (specializing in video games, anime, and internet memes), and messing around with my cat CC (Crazy Cat/Cary's Cat, depending who you ask).
